About This App

Welcome to your personal Book Collection App — a simple and elegant way to track what you've read and keep your library organized.

Whether you're an avid reader or just starting your literary journey, this app lets you add books, rate them, and view your full reading history — all in one clean, accessible place.

The app is built using modern web technologies including Python (Flask), SQLAlchemy, Flask-WTF, and Bootstrap 5. It’s lightweight, fast, and easy to use.

Created by a passionate computer science student, this project is a learning journey and a tool for readers who value simplicity and clarity in tracking their reading goals.
